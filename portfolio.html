My Art Portfolio

Kalamkari Culture

Watercolor & Pen on paper, 2024. This piece was created to represent the intersection of a specific South Indian art style, Kalamkari, and the symbolism of birds from other cultures.

The Musician

Watercolor & Pen on paper, 2023. This piece is an exploration of color theory and one of my first portraits.

Marigolds

Watercolor on paper, 2024. I created this piece to represent the profound significance of flowers in my culture, and how they are almost present everywhere.

School Lunch

Watercolor on paper, 2024. This piece symbolizes the intersection of the school lunches of various cultures.

Waterfall

Watercolor on paper, 2022. This was my first attempt at painting a more abstract, implied version of a landscape.

Boats in Greece

Watercolor on paper, 2023. This was a more advanced attempt at representing water and reflections.

Illumination

Watercolor & Pen on paper, 2024. This piece was created during times of global conflict in order to represent the unity of religion.

Dogwoods

Watercolor on paper, 2022. Inspired by flowers in my backyard, I wanted to practice negative painting and creating dark backgrounds in watercolor.

The Guitarist

Watercolor on paper, 2023. This piece was my first portrait, allowing me to go outside of my comfort zone.
